Visualization of action lists with relations

We can visualize dependency relations of 50 short sentences from the Dumas corpus along with each words' "action lists" and POS tags; the arrow labels contain the "action list" of the dependant.

Note that displaCy doesn't explicitly show the root relation. This means that the root of the sentence in this type of visualization is always the word that has no head within the sentence.

The relations and action lists were predicted by IncPar and are not necessarily correct. For example, the results seem to have predicted multiple root words in some cases.
